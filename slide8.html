Challenges and Engineering Limitations of Adaptive AUTOSAR

Key obstacles in adopting the Adaptive Platform

High Computational Requirements

  • Needs multi-core CPUs, powerful ECUs
  • Cost + thermal management become critical

Complexity of SOA Architecture

  • Dynamic service discovery
  • Synchronization & timing non-determinism

Safety Certification (ISO 26262)

  • Dynamic behavior makes certification harder
  • Non-static scheduling complicates validation

Cybersecurity Risks

  • OTA updates increase attack surface
  • Requires secure boot, encryption, intrusion detection

Integration with Legacy Systems

  • Hybrid architectures (Classic + Adaptive)
  • Cross-communication can be difficult

Large Software Development Effort

  • Requires new toolchains (Vector, EB, ETAS)
  • Engineers need OS + networking expertise
